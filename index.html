About Me!

From there I started taking electronic engineering courses to advance myself. During that timeframe I got a wife and a kid on the way, so I became a stay at home dad and worked on the weekends. Eventually moved back to South Carolina and became a Maintenance Technician with a focus on Kuka Robotics.
